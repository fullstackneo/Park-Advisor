Blue Ridge
Parkway

A Blue Ridge Parkway experience is
unlike any other: a slow-paced and
relaxing drive revealing stunning
long-range vistas and close-up views
of the rugged mountains and pastoral
landscapes of the Appalachian
Highlands. The Parkway meanders for
469 miles, protecting a diversity of
plants and animals, and providing
opportunities for enjoying all that
makes this region of the country so
special.
